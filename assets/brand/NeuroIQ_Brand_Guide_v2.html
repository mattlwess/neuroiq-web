Brand
Foundation

Mission

To give people with damaged or underperforming brains the tools to rebuild, retain, and reclaim their cognitive potential — using the hours they are already sleeping.

The three-layer product

Layer 1 — Binaural entrainment. User selects frequency. Brain is tuned to target state (delta, theta, alpha, gamma). The neural window opens.

Layer 2 — Subliminal delivery. Content is encoded and delivered below the conscious auditory threshold in timed intervals matched to the brain's processing cycle.

Layer 3 — User dataset. Any content the user defines — notes, documents, audio transcripts, study materials. The payload is theirs.

02
The Pause
Mark

What it is

The mark between "Neuro" and "IQ" is a sleep spindle — the exact EEG waveform signature of memory consolidation during NREM sleep. It appears as a burst of neural oscillation (12–15 Hz) that waxes, peaks, and wanes over 0.5–3 seconds.

This is not decoration. NeuroIQ delivers data packets timed to these natural intervals. The pause between delivery cycles is when the hippocampus encodes what it just received. The logo shows the product working.

The three-word explanation

Data
DELIVERED
Pause
ENCODES
Retained
LEARNED

The pause is not dead air. It is the moment of active neurological encoding. NeuroIQ's intentional inter-stimulus intervals — built around natural sleep spindle timing — are what separates it from every other audio delivery system.

Spindle anatomy — what the mark depicts

Spindle Purple — #534AB7
The pause mark uses a distinct purple — separate from the signal blues — to signal a different register: not the data, not the outcome, but the mechanism between them. It is the only purple element in the entire brand system.

Voice
& Tone
Trait 01
Clinically confident
Every claim is grounded in neuroscience. We don't hedge. The mechanism is real — we say so.
"Binaural beats entrain your brain to a target frequency. During theta state, memory consolidation and new pathway formation peak. NeuroIQ delivers your content precisely at this window."

Trait 02
Hopeful, not hype
Our users have been told their cognitive limits are permanent. We reject that — but with evidence, not promises. Hope built on mechanism is credible. Hope built on marketing is not.
"Your brain repairs itself every night. NeuroIQ ensures that repair process also builds new pathways to the knowledge you need."

Respects intelligence
Our core user survived brain trauma. They don't need to be spoken down to. Use precise terminology. Trust them.

Quiet confidence
The product works while people sleep. The brand follows that lead. Short sentences. Active verbs. No exclamation marks. No emoji in clinical contexts.
"Session complete. 8h 14m at 6.5 Hz. Dataset: Project Brief v3. Sleep quality: strong."
"Amazing session!! You crushed it tonight! Your brain is supercharged!! 🧠⚡🚀"

UI Design Principles

Dark by default
The app runs during sleep. Low stimulation, dark surfaces, no jarring whites before bed. Night mode is the only mode.
The pause is visible
The waveform display shows data delivery gaps in Spindle Purple — the user can see the interval happening. The pause is not hidden, it is the hero of the UI.
Data is precise
JetBrains Mono for all session data — Hz, duration, pass count, dataset ID. The user should feel they are operating precision equipment, not a wellness app.
Green means something
Recovery Green appears only on positive outcomes. Every other UI element is blue. When green appears, the brain did something measurable. That restraint is the reward system.
Minimal pre-sleep interaction
Select dataset. Select frequency. Put the phone down. NeuroIQ runs the rest. The session setup flow should require no more than three taps.
